```shell
usage: ipo [-p PAST_DAYS] [-f FUTURE_DAYS]
```

Past and future IPOs. [Source: https://finnhub.io]

* -p : Number of past days to look for IPOs. Default 0.
* -f : Number of future days to look for IPOs. Default 10.
